Abstract:

The United States is the home of the highest rate of gun ownership worldwide due to safeguarding by the Second Amendment to the U.S. Constitution.
Individuals with firearms find usage through various activities, such as self-defense, hunting, and target shooting.
Although there are several harmless use cases for firearms, there are many harmful ones as well.
In this narrative visualization you will be able to explore some other statistics that demonstrate the violence caused by individuals who own a firearm in America.
The source for the dataset used in this visualization can be found at this link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/eimadevyni/shooting-1982-2023-cleaned.
