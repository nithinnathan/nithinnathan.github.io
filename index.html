About

The United States is the home of the highest rate of gun ownership worldwide due to the freedom to bear arms.
Although there are several harmless effects of firearms, there are many harmful ones as well, such as gun violence.
In this narrative visualization you will be able to explore statistics that demonstrate the violence caused by individuals who own a firearm in America and how that violence has increased over several decades.
The source for the dataset used in this visualization can be found at this link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/eimadevyni/shooting-1982-2023-cleaned.
This narrative visualization was created by Nithin Nathan (nnatha3@illinois.edu) as part of the CS 416: Data Visualization class at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign.
