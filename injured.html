Injured

An injury is defined as "any physiological damage to living tissue caused by immediate stress".
In the case of gun violence, injuries refer to individuals affected by gun-related events but not resulting in fatalities.
The below line chart shows the sum of individuals injured over time.
The X-axis represents the different years while the Y-axis represents sum of individuals injured for that year.
In order to view the total sum of individuals injured from the 1980s to 2020s, hover over any part of the chart.
